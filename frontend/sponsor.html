Native, not bolted on.

Phylax could not exist as a stack of loosely-wired services. The whole product is a trust boundary with a state machine and a live stream — exactly the shape InsForge has.

The thesis

Phylax is built natively on InsForge. InsForge creates the trust boundaries, hosts the long-running protected-compute services, enforces access controls, persists the auditable decision ledger, streams the live incident response, stores private artifacts, and deploys the control plane.
